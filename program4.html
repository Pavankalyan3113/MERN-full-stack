Welcome to Our Hotel Restaurant

Explore our exquisite range of dishes

Signature Dish: Royal Lobster Feast

Our Royal Lobster Feast is the ultimate indulgence, featuring fresh, succulent lobsters served with garlic butter, a side of creamy mashed potatoes, and a choice of vegetables. Perfect for seafood lovers.

Vegetarian Special: Garden Delight

For our vegetarian guests, the Garden Delight offers a colorful mix of grilled vegetables, quinoa, and a homemade balsamic glaze. A healthy yet flavorful option for those who prefer plant-based dishes.

Classic Dish: Grilled Steak

Our Grilled Steak is a timeless favorite, cooked to your desired level of doneness, accompanied by crispy fries and a rich peppercorn sauce. The perfect meal for meat lovers.

Soup of the Day: Tomato Basil Soup

Our Tomato Basil Soup is made with vine-ripened tomatoes, fresh basil, and a touch of cream, offering a hearty and comforting starter to any meal.

Side Dish: Crispy Onion Rings

Our crispy onion rings are made from fresh onions, lightly battered and fried until golden brown. Served with a tangy dipping sauce, these are the perfect addition to any main dish.

Special Dessert: Chocolate Lava Cake

End your meal with our delectable Chocolate Lava Cake, filled with molten chocolate and served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. A truly indulgent dessert for chocolate lovers.
